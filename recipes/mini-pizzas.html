Mini pizzas


Ingredients

Dough:

  • flour - 450 g
  • milk - 300 ml
  • fast-acting yeast - 6 g
  • sunflower oil - 3 tbsp. l.
  • sugar - 2 tbsp. l.
  • salt - 1 tsp

Filling:

  • tomato sauce
  • cherry tomatoes, red and yellow - 10-12 pcs.
  • basil - 4-5 sprigs
  • pitted olives - 120 g
  • mozzarella - 10 balls

Cooking method

  1. Stir sugar and yeast in slightly warm milk, cover the container with a towel and leave for 10 minutes.
  2. Then we sift the flour, add salt and vegetable oil, knead the dough, roll it into a lump and put it in a warm place for half an hour.
  3. Cut the mozzarella and tomatoes into circles, olives into rings.
  4. Tear the basil leaves with your hands.
  5. We divide the dough into 8 parts, roll out cakes, grease each with tomato sauce.
  6. Spread mixed cheese, tomatoes and olives, send to bake in the oven at 200 Â° C for 15 minutes.

Garnish the mini pizzas with basil leaves before serving.
