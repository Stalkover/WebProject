Quesadilla


Ingredients

  • cheese cakes tortillas - 12 pcs.
  • canned corn - 400 g
  • onion - 2 pcs.
  • hard cheese - 200 g
  • olive oil - for frying
  • tomato sauce - 2 tbsp. l.
  • salt and ground chili - to taste.

Cooking method

  1. We rub the cheese on a coarse grater.
  2. Cut the onion into small cubes and fry in olive oil until golden brown.
  3. Pour the corn kernels, add the tomato sauce, salt and pepper, mix well.
  4. Put the tortilla on a hot grill pan.
  5. Sprinkle one half with cheese, lay out a little filling, sprinkle with cheese again, cover with the second half and press with a spatula.
  6. Brown the cake until golden stripes and turn over.
  7. We prepare the rest of the cakes in the same way.
